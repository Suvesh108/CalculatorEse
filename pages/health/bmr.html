📖 About BMR Formulas

Mifflin-St Jeor (1990)

Men: (10 × kg) + (6.25 × cm) − (5 × age) + 5
Women: (10 × kg) + (6.25 × cm) − (5 × age) − 161

Harris-Benedict (Revised 1984)

Men: 88.362 + (13.397 × kg) + (4.799 × cm) − (5.677 × age)
Women: 447.593 + (9.247 × kg) + (3.098 × cm) − (4.330 × age)
